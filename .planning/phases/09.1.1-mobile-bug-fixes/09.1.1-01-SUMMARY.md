### Active plugins reviewed

- `classy-wp` (v1.0.0) — loads Classy SDK script, no popup code
- `insert-headers-and-footers` (v2.3.4) — active but no configured content
- `gravityforms` (v2.9.27) — donation forms, no popup functionality

### Conclusion

**The "Make a difference!" popup is Classy's abandoned cart nudge feature, injected client-side by the Classy SDK JavaScript** (`https://giving.classy.org/embedded/api/sdk/js/{org_id}`).

This is a Classy platform feature that triggers when a user starts filling out the donation form but doesn't complete it. It appears as a modal/overlay with "Make a difference!" messaging encouraging them to complete the donation.

**Actionable next step for Plan 02:** Log in to the Classy organization dashboard and look for abandoned cart / email nudge / conversion settings to disable this feature. This may be under "Campaigns" > campaign settings, or organization-level "Fundraising" settings.
